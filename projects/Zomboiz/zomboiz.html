ZomboiZ: survive 10th Jul 2023
Game
University
Latest University project, a classic top down fast action survival shooter. Run around, explore the city full of Zombies, collect weapons and gear and slay your way though. My work: Game Design Documentation, Game Design, Coding, Custom Shaders, Character modeling & Animation, UI, VFX

Visuals

Materials & Shaders

To make all characters appealing and to make them stand out I made a custom shader using Shader Graph and Code. The characters have a Rim Light that reacts to lighting around them. It works great with Day and Night System, various light sources from street lights to explosions in combat.

The faces have a glow, so you can see them even at night, and it gives them a bit of spookiness.

And all the zombies use the same material, which also includes blood, bones and the black outline around them, with GPU instancing the performance is easily obtained.

Environment & Lighting

Day and Night system, which make the game feel alive and dynamic. Lens flares and other effects that make the action feel flashy.

VFX

Particle systems are important, and they make things happening feel impactful.Weapons feel powerful and fun to shoot, enemies explode on death in many parts which makes them a lot of fun to kill.

Animations

Characters have animation states, player has multiple landing animations depending on the force of the fall, weapons have their own animations that are implemented with animation layers.

UI

It is simple in looks, but hand designed with animations that make them fun and intuitive.

Game Design & Code

Player

The player controls are the most complicated as they do all the movement, shooting, camera controls and inventory system. The character is fast and responsive and takes priority over everything else performance wise.

Enemies and Spawning

2 types of enemies with different kind of AI. Navigation Mesh Agent based regular zombies and Custom Physics based fat rolling zombies.

Optimization

For an Agent based AI to run smoothly I have implemented multiple features.There is a Zombie Manager object that controls all the enemies, it runs on a separate thread for best performance, it also manages spawning and de-spawning of enemies, so only the ones near the players view are working, they de-spawn depending on the distance from the player and new ones are spawned when needed.

Spawning

I made a custom tool for Unity Editor which allows the level designer to easily place and manipulate spawn points for enemies. Their size and difficulty level can be changed, with visual feedback for Level Designers.

Made with Forward Planning

Enemies, weapons, pick ups, Player, everything is made with forward planning. Everything is modular and made with proper Object Oriented approach.This allows adding features to be fast, easy and as painless as possible!
